Lake Albert

As the sun rises, take to the mirror-like waters of Lake Albert, and search for the coveted shoebill stork in the marshy shorelines. Continue exploring with mountain biking over craggy hills, and end the day with a magical bush dinner and a night game drive.

Lake Bunyonyi

Protruding from translucent water, luscious stepped hills provide a natural staircase leading you to some of the best views in Uganda. Formed over 10,000 years ago, when a river was dammed by lava, Lake Bunyonyi is rich with history and bird song.
